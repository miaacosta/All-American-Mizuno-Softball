About All American Softball School

Founded nearly 25 years ago, All-American Softball, Inc. is a privately-owned business directed by collegiate and professional softball standout, Kelly Jackson. We are the biggest business of our kind in the area, as well as the most personal and accommodating to our athletes' needs. We offer private and group lessons for ages six and up in all areas of softball as well as several camps and clinics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to motivate and teach young athletes the fundamentals of competition and discipline and to provide a positive environment for the development of successful athletic careers.

Our Instructors

Kelly Jackson
Owner, Founder and President

Kelly Jackson has been a leading presence in the world of softball for over 30 years. Anchoring the team at first base for Arizona State University's Sun Devils during her college years, Kelly took her experience into the teaching arena by establishing All American Softball, Inc. As President, Owner and a student of the game, Kelly brings insurmountable expertise and experience to All American Softball


Keri Casas
Director of Operations

Keri Casas is the Director of Operations as well as lead instructor at All-American Softball, Inc. Beginning her softball career in private lessons through All-American Softball at the age of 9, Keri worked her way into the College Prep Program with aspirations of a collegiate softball scholarship. Thanks to her dedication to lessons, the game, and academics, Keri was awarded a Division 1 scholarship to Syracuse University, graduating with academic honors as a pitcher and hitter.

About Them
All of our instructors give lessons in pitching, hitting, catching, and defense. Each member of our staff is trained by Kelly Jackson to ensure that they impart the same quality of information and fundamentals to our students. Each of our instructors have graduated from the All-American College Prep program and have had successful athletic careers playing college-level softball.
